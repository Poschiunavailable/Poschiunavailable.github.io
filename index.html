About Me

Passionate Game Developer

Hello! My name is Patrick Temborius and I am a Game Programmer. I am passionate about physics, games, ai, procedural generation and cutting edge technology. I have experience in various Game Engines and programming languages. In my free time, I enjoy watching lectures, woodworking, enjoy oldschool lan-parties with my friends or go to the cinema.

With a strong background in programming, I am constantly learning and adapting to new technologies to stay on the cutting edge of the industry, always looking forward to new technologies and releases from both, Unity and Unreal Engine. I'm constantly trying out new workflows, tools and programming patterns and love learning from or teaching to colleagues. I consider myself an empathic person and have experience with distributing tasks to other team members and quickly figure out possible issues and problems that slow down production, trying to find solutions for those that everybody is satisfied with.
